Curry Chicken

Description

This dish is absoultely delicous. It has white rice, veggies, and chicken it.

It is also very spicy so make sure to drink alot of water when consuming.

Ingredients:

  • Chicken
  • White Rice
  • Peppers

Steps:

  1. Wash and Season Chicken
  2. Add into pot
  3. Cook
  4. Serve
